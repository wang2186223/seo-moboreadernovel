The Horned One

We both looked at each other in disbelief, but she was owed It seemed she meant to collect.

I *knew* I shouldn't have let her sit in my chair but she looked so cute on it.

He held his face in shock.

"LIKE *YOU* can pull ANY tone with me!" She screamed. "You literally made me without anyone's consent, then you get mad when he didn't want me as a gift! AND YOU of *all* people had the nerve to be upset that someone you MADE AS A FUCKING PARTY FAVOR DECIDED WHO TO LET JUMP IN HER BOX!!"

"Dear that's enou-"

She slapped me too.

. It actually hurt..

I bled.

She was channeling my throne!

"You will fix this." She hissed. "You both will fucking fix this or so help me I WILL turn the realms inside out. Do. Not. Test. ME."

"I don't have to-" Arviel's belligerent attitude was *changed*.

"YOU WILL FUCKING FIX THIS!"

Her face morphed into an amalgamation of multiple animals as she snarled in his face.

Her flesh was made unstable, and as hot as this was, I didn't know what to do about it She's only ever been my mate The only thing I could think of was 'the dark side of the moon' when I looked at her summon claws as long as swords.

After all she was made with no weapon so now she made her own

Her robes before they ripped became a beautiful red, black flowered kimono. The black jacket trailed to the floor, and the wide sleeves ended at her knees. The entire jacket had a haunted forest depicted on the bottom, and there were full moons on either sleeve; both of which were red.Her silver hair was black, but faded to red as well

I'd never seen her do this Not even for herself

Not stand up for herself

but how could she back then? As a lesser goddess captive, the smallest desire for her to die could kill her I bet he had barriers around himself to shield her from his anger then, so he could keep her hostage

Even that was my fault I should have made her come to my realm If I'd have commanded it back then, Xaxas would have been born in the castle.

But I'd have been no better than him.

She became a personification of the night itself. She had bat wings and blades were her claws, being shaped like a bear's Great horned owl feathers were overtop the webbing, while she had a black and red pattern A wolf's head and torso complimented owl talons. This would have been fun for *me* if she also wasn't mad at me. Though she had no horns. She was beautiful.

She was thin and elegant. Her silhouette was of an owl feathered wolf with owl tufts as horns. Her tail was nearly as long as her.

Her legs were long and thin like a deer's, but the claws were silver swords.

I knew this would happen, and I let it to fuck them both.

"I know how to fix this." I said pulling her close.

"LET ME GO!!!" She screamed and I held her. "It isn't fair!" She hit my chest until she clawed my robes. ".When you were gone. He never once added to my troublesHumblon did his job, and *you killed him for it.*" She chewed it until she changed back, and I used the scraps of my robes to cover her. I could make more, but I really did need to fix this.

"Lulliba is right dear That's why I tried stopping you Humblon, even if he was brought back would never forgive you." Said Lia.

".He will He Fuck. Why'd you let me do it?" Arviel demanded. "I can't blame you it's our nature to be this way."

"Yeah, and I should have been better." I said feeling like shit

Lulliba wouldn't look at me Her red eyes now cried blood, as every moon on every world was likely either red, or would eclipse soon

She wouldn't even look at me She even contemplated our bond as did Lia.

"I can't lose you little mate. Even as the Only Moon Goddess you are still too weak to use such power." I said unable to heal the soul burns already blistering She's going to really hurt for doing this, and I'm going to feel like shit for forever. "Please wish it."

"I will wish for Nothing." Lulliba said angrily. "You both did this; *You* resolve this."

. Damn.

"Then I'll do it for free." I said to appease my most precious. "Arviel, if I bring him back as a god, he doesn't want to do it anymore. We're going to have to make a new Mercy, and the old one will have to be mortal?"

"IF you do that, then Humblon will eventually die again, and the new mercy will not want to do it"

"We could fuse them together into a two headed-"

"No." Everyone disagreed.

"Nobody Fucking Quits because they don't want to do their Fucking Job!" Screamed my eldest.

Xaxashevaal had Humblon by the throat, and stuffed him in his body like you're trying to put on a sock The powers were grabbed off the table and shoved down Humblon's throat as well.

Humblon gasped for air. "Master please!" He begged. "Don't' take me from her!"

"Huh? Why would I do that?" He still held the soul by the scruff. "Nayamya right?" he picked up the dust and remade the mortal, though some of her was lost, so she was a centimeter smaller.

Humblon cradled her sleeping form as I do my Lulliba. how could I not see it

She was not his mate, but he cared for her as one. A platonic love

"You would do that Judgement?" He asked. "You even kept her the way I made her"

"A million times." Said my son. "You both have 10 seconds to explain why Mercy's tears blessed my altar."

"We were owed for his actions. To do NOTHING would ruin the alignment." Said Arviel, a little flustered and agitated from being called out.

As the Judge, he was not informed on such a large action so he was owed as we were..

He was owed an explanation, and he wasn't satisfied with our answer

"And instead, you both got Bagel Killed. You made me Fucking Lose My Streak to a Mortal." He hissed, referring to a shooting game he plays with my nephew, War.

"What?" We both asked.

"NO NOT BAGEL!!!!" Screamed Lulliba.

"I like that lil' scamp." Said Lia. "If you don't fix this with him, you're not coming home."

"And you better not try to replace her with someone else's either." Growled Lulliba, and they fist bumped.

. Where did my butterfly go..? I didn't like having a tiny *vicious* tiger

"I think that's fair." Said Xaxashevaal smugly. His grin at us, me especially, could eat shit!

"I take it that was one of the Charred family pets." Asked Arviel, and the girls nodded.

"Lulliba sends me videos of her mortals. I think that one is my favorite. She's so cheeky." Giggled Lia.

A black werewolf playfully chased the little brown pit mix because she had his pants. It thinks the former crown prince is her 'papa'. My heart sank as I still couldn't find her.

It calls them mama and papa.

***I'm fucked.***

I'm so fucked. *Why did I do this to myself?* *I just had to get what I was owed.*

.This was much too cute to be sending around.

They have *mortal attachment* to this creature.

"Oh yeah, you sent me that one yesterday." Said Arviel, inwardly freaking out with me... [Zuviel we're cooked as the fucking sacrificial turkey on Thankful Day.] he said so only I would understand.

[Fried as an eggbound hen on Sunday.] I said back.

..

*But When did they get a damn group chat?!*

The eldest living mortal blood of her Herold was that wolf. I realize that now Kaiden Charred, Kaizen's other grandfather.

. She really . *Really* likes that line. SHIT.

I didn't know if I *could* fix it Purgatory was down Hell's gates are flooded I looked for a dog I'd never paid attention to, but I couldn't cheat.

If I don't find it, nor Arviel, we'll Have to keep looking

It's bad enough I got in hot water for Humblon

or the countless trillions Not everyone or everything got back up.

Double Shit. This is why I don't do things on impulse. All it took was 2 seconds Just that for 81% of all the worlds to be shaken to their foundations.

"You know how much of an earful Harmony gave me? I should have Never Gifted her." He growled in a rant while I still looked. "I see your face. I think she's gone. I had to trade the dog of Kaiden to reset the entire planet because you two were involved. You're Welcome."

"I am so thankful I'm not one planet anymore." Said Lia holding her forehead, and my mate did too.

"Humblon you already have a Herold, so I now place my judgment on this mortal. God's magic has touched this one, so here. I'm making the damn mate bond myself." Xaxashevaal waved his hand. "The goddess of Fucking I Don't Know, Uh The Goddess of Forgiveness."

"I like that." Said Humblon.

"That's your wife now; all apologies go to her. Since she's your wife, she's protected. Now she's a Minor goddess that doesn't even need worship, and she can stay your High Priestess. Now stop making fucking drama." He pointed a clawed finger at us. "You're all wasting my fucking time. Oh, and every mortal who can't come back, even the animals must be compensated 1 gold for every day they should have lived." Growled my son. I was proud as any father could ever be even though the chastising was meant for me too.

"Forgive me brother I-" Humblon cut off Arviel.

"I am Humblon." He said firmly. "Do not let an apology crease your lips your highness."

That was *cold*. The glare given to me by my mate was just as

"I won't either." I growled. I was more displeased at letting my little Lulliba reshape herself in anger

"Nobody Fucking Asked You For It." Xaxas said on the wind, since he disappeared.

"You little- you know what. I'll be the bigger god and accept that." I growled.

As I found the soul of the little dog to return I realized something:

I was so Hellbent, pun not intended, on being compensated now I must compensate more than what I was given.

So if I'd have left well enough alone, none of this would have happenedAll this took was 2 mortal seconds What a mess I've made.
